# VentShield — Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Frame (One-Time Purchase)

| SKU | MSRP | COGS Target | Gross Margin Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Small (6x10) | $15 | $3-5 | 65-80% |
| Medium (6x12) | $18 | $3.50-5.50 | 69-81% |
| Large (10x10/12x12) | $22-25 | $4-6 | 73-84% |

COGS includes: injection-molded frame, embedded magnets, packaging, insert. Does not include shipping, marketing, or platform fees.

### 2. Filter Insert Refills (Recurring)

| Pack Size | MSRP | COGS Target | Gross Margin Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4-pack | $12 | $2-3 | 75-83% |
| 8-pack | $20 | $3.50-5 | 75-83% |
| 12-pack (bulk) | $28 | $5-7 | 75-82% |

Replacement cycle: every 30-90 days per filter. Household with 3 filtered vents = 12-36 inserts/year.

### 3. Subscription (Auto-Ship)

| Plan | Frequency | Price | Discount vs. One-Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter (4 inserts) | Quarterly | $10/quarter | ~17% |
| Standard (8 inserts) | Quarterly | $17/quarter | ~15% |
| Household (12 inserts) | Quarterly | $24/quarter | ~14% |

Subscription reduces CAC amortization period and increases LTV. Target: 30% of customers on subscription by month 6.

### 4. Travel Kit (Bundle)

| Contents | MSRP | COGS Target |
|---|---|---|
| 1 frame (Small) + 4 inserts + carrying pouch | $30 | $8-12 |
| 1 frame (Medium) + 4 inserts + carrying pouch | $35 | $9-13 |
| Multi-size kit (Small + Medium frames, 8 inserts, pouch) | $55 | $15-20 |

## Unit Economics

### Per-Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Assumptions**:
- Average frame purchase: $18
- Insert replacement: $12/quarter
- Retention: 60% at 12 months, 35% at 24 months
- Subscription discount: 15%

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Cumulative |
|---|---|---|---|
| Frame revenue | $18 | $0 | $18 |
| Insert revenue (non-sub) | $33.60 | $20.16 | $53.76 |
| Insert revenue (sub) | $28.56 | $17.14 | $45.70 |
| **Blended LTV** | **$48-52** | **$17-20** | **$65-72** |

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Targets

| Channel | Target CAC | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Amazon PPC | $8-12 | High intent, competitive |
| Meta/Instagram ads | $10-15 | Visual product, good for demos |
| Influencer (micro, allergy niche) | $5-8 | High trust, lower CPM |
| Organic/SEO | $2-4 | Long-term, content-driven |
| Reddit/forums | $3-6 | Community trust, low spend |

**Target blended CAC**: under $10. LTV:CAC ratio target: 5:1+.

### Margin Stack (Steady State)

| Line Item | % of Revenue |
|---|---|
| COGS (frame + inserts) | 20-25% |
| Shipping/fulfillment | 10-15% |
| Platform fees (Amazon/Shopify) | 8-15% |
| Marketing/CAC | 15-20% |
| **Gross margin after fulfillment** | **45-55%** |
| **Net margin target** | **15-25%** |

## Sales Channels

### Phase 1 (Launch)
- Amazon FBA — primary discovery channel
- Shopify DTC — higher margin, subscription hub
- Etsy — handmade/niche health products audience

### Phase 2 (Growth)
- Airbnb host marketplace / partnerships
- Hotel procurement (B2B)
- Allergy/asthma specialty retailers (online)

### Phase 3 (Scale)
- Retail (Home Depot, Target, Bed Bath & Beyond successor)
- Property management companies (bulk B2B)
- International expansion (UK, Australia, SE Asia — high humidity markets)

## Breakeven Analysis

| Scenario | Monthly Units | Monthly Revenue | Monthly Costs | Breakeven? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 500 frames + 1,000 inserts | $21,000 | ~$18,000 | Near breakeven |
| Moderate | 1,500 frames + 4,000 inserts | $75,000 | ~$52,000 | Profitable |
| Aggressive | 5,000 frames + 15,000 inserts | $270,000 | ~$175,000 | Strong margin |

**Fixed costs to cover**: tooling amortization (~$15-30K injection mold), inventory, software/subscriptions, part-time labor.
