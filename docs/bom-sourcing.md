## 10. Cyanoacrylate Glue (for bonding magnets to TPU)

| Product | Source | Price | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|-------|
| [Loctite 406 (20g)](https://us.amazon.com/LOCTITE-406-Super-Glue-Adhesive/dp/B07H2YGBTJ) | Amazon | ~$23 | The standard for plastic/elastomer bonding. Low viscosity, wicking grade — seeps into gaps around embedded magnets. Fast fixture time. |
| [Loctite 406 (20g)](https://www.ellsworth.com/products/adhesives/cyanoacrylate/henkel-loctite-406-surface-insensitive-instant-adhesive-clear-20-g-bottle/) | Ellsworth | ~$23 | Same product, industrial supplier. Reliable stock. |
| [Gorilla Super Glue Gel (15g)](https://www.amazon.com) | Amazon | ~$6-8 | Budget alternative. Gel formula resists running on flexible surfaces. Less ideal than 406 for TPU specifically but workable. |

**Recommendation:** Loctite 406 is purpose-built for plastics and elastomers (TPU is a thermoplastic polyurethane elastomer). The low viscosity wicks into magnet pockets printed with slight clearance. For best results, pair with Loctite 770 primer (~$20) on the TPU surface — dramatically improves bond strength on polyolefins and difficult plastics. If embedding magnets during the print (pause-and-insert method), CA is mainly insurance — the printed layers above the magnet do most of the retention.
