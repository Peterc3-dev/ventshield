FAQ

Questions.

Does it reduce airflow?

Minimally. MERV 8-11 media is designed for residential HVAC pressure drops. You shouldn't notice a difference in room temperature or comfort. We test with an anemometer to verify.

Will it fit my vent?

Three sizes cover roughly 80% of US residential and hotel registers. Small (6x10"), Medium (6x12"), Large (10-12" square). If your vent is metal, magnets hold it. If plastic, the friction grip handles it.

Can I wash the filter inserts?

No. The antimicrobial treatment and media structure degrade when wet. Replace them — that's what the color indicator is for. The frame is hand-washable with mild soap.

How often do I replace inserts?

Every 30-90 days depending on your air quality, humidity, and HVAC runtime. The color-change strip takes the guesswork out — when it's brown, swap it.

Is it noisy?

No. The filter media actually dampens turbulence slightly. If anything, it's quieter than a bare vent.
