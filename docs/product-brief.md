# VentShield — Product Brief

## Overview

VentShield is a reusable, collapsible vent filter frame with replaceable antimicrobial filter inserts. It attaches to standard residential and commercial HVAC supply registers without tools or permanent modification.

## SKU Sizing

| SKU | Dimensions | Target Vents | Est. Coverage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Small | 6" x 10" | Standard supply registers | ~40% of installs |
| Medium | 6" x 12" | Common rectangular ceiling/wall | ~25% of installs |
| Large | 10" x 10" / 12" x 12" | Square ceiling registers | ~15% of installs |

These three sizes cover approximately 80% of US residential and hotel vent registers based on standard HVAC sizing (see market research for register dimension survey).

## Frame

### Material Options

| Material | Pros | Cons | Est. Unit Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) | Flexible, folds flat, overmoldable with magnets, good temp range | Slightly heavier | $1.50-2.50 |
| Silicone | Excellent grip, high temp tolerance, food-safe perception | Higher tooling cost, harder to embed magnets | $2.00-3.50 |
| Flexible polypropylene | Cheapest, lightweight, good chemical resistance | Less grippy, living hinge fatigue over time | $0.80-1.50 |

**Recommended for prototype**: TPE — best balance of grip, foldability, and magnet embedding.

### Collapsible Design

The frame uses a living-hinge fold pattern (similar to collapsible silicone travel cups). Folds to approximately 30% of deployed footprint. Target packed thickness: under 15mm including one filter insert.

### Attachment System

**Dual-mode, no tools required:**

1. **Magnetic (metal registers)**: 4-8 embedded N52 neodymium disc magnets (6mm x 2mm) positioned at corners and midpoints. Pull force per magnet: ~1.5 lbs. Total hold: 6-12 lbs — sufficient for ceiling mounting against gravity with filter weight (~20g).

2. **Friction grip (plastic registers)**: Silicone or TPE lip with slight interference fit. The frame's outer edge has a tapered channel that grips the register louver frame. Works by compression — no adhesive, no damage.

Both modes active simultaneously. On metal registers, magnets do the heavy lifting. On plastic, friction grip handles it.

## Filter Insert

### Media Specification

| Parameter | Spec |
|---|---|
| MERV rating | 8-11 (MERV 13 tested but likely too restrictive for residential supply) |
| Media type | Synthetic (polyester or polypropylene meltblown) |
| Antimicrobial treatment | Silver ion or zinc pyrithione embedded in media fibers |
| Pressure drop | ≤0.08" w.g. at 500 FPM face velocity (must not noticeably reduce airflow) |
| Thickness | 3-5mm |
| Replacement interval | 30-90 days depending on conditions |

### Saturation Indicator

A color-change strip bonded to the downstream face of the filter insert:

- **White**: fresh / low loading
- **Yellow**: moderate loading, still functional
- **Brown/dark**: saturated, replace

Mechanism: pH-sensitive or moisture-reactive dye that responds to accumulated particulate and biofilm byproducts. Similar chemistry to indoor air quality indicator cards (e.g., Mold Armor test strips).

### Insert Attachment

Friction-fit into frame channel. No clips or adhesive. Insert slides in from one edge, frame tension holds it flat. Designed for 10-second swap.

## Environmental Specs

| Parameter | Range |
|---|---|
| Operating temp | 40°F - 140°F (HVAC supply air range) |
| Humidity tolerance | 0-95% RH non-condensing |
| UV resistance | Not required (indoor use only) |
| Washability (frame) | Hand wash with mild soap, air dry |
| Washability (filter) | Not washable — replace |

## Open Questions

- Antimicrobial treatment longevity over 90 days in high-humidity environments
- Whether color-change indicator chemistry exists that's specific enough to be useful (vs. just getting dirty)
- Regulatory: does an antimicrobial-treated consumer product need EPA registration?
