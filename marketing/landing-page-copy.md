## FAQ

**Does it reduce airflow?**
Minimally. MERV 8-11 media is designed for residential HVAC pressure drops. You shouldn't notice a difference in room temperature or comfort.

**Will it work on my vent?**
Three sizes cover ~80% of US residential and hotel registers. If your vent is metal, magnets hold it. If plastic, the friction grip handles it. If the louvers are recessed, the frame's channel accommodates up to 15mm depth.

**Is it loud?**
No. The filter media dampens turbulence slightly. If anything, it's quieter.

**Can I wash the filter inserts?**
No — the antimicrobial treatment and media structure degrade when wet. Replace them. The frame is hand-washable.

**How do I know when to replace?**
The color-change indicator strip on the filter goes from white to yellow to brown as particulate accumulates. When it's brown, swap it.
